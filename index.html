Popular Destinations In Rajasthan

Must Visit

Hawa Mahal

Jaipur

Hawa Mahal

Built in 1799, this "Palace of Winds" allowed royal ladies to observe street festivals unseen.

  • Highlights: 953 Jharokhas
  • Best Time: Oct - Mar
  • Entry: ₹50 (Indian)
History

Mehrangarh Fort

Jodhpur

Mehrangarh Fort

One of the largest forts in India, rising 410 feet above the city of Jodhpur.

  • Highlights: Museum & Flying Fox
  • Best Time: Nov - Feb
  • Entry: ₹100 (Indian)
Royal

City Palace

Udaipur

City Palace

A magnificent complex of 11 palaces built over 400 years, offering panoramic views of Lake Pichola.

  • Highlights: Crystal Gallery & Museum
  • Best Time: Sep - Mar
  • Entry: ₹400 (Museum)
Desert

Jaisalmer Fort

Jaisalmer

Sonar Quila

One of the few "living forts" in the world, made of yellow sandstone that glows gold at sunset.

  • Highlights: Jain Temples & Havelis
  • Best Time: Nov - Feb
  • Entry: ₹50 (Indian)
Wildlife

Ranthambore

Sawai Madhopur

Tiger Reserve

A former royal hunting ground, now a famous wildlife sanctuary known for its Bengal Tigers.

  • Highlights: Jeep Safari & Fort
  • Best Time: Oct - Jun
  • Entry: ~₹1300 (Safari)
Spiritual

Pushkar Lake

Pushkar

Sacred Lake

A prominent Hindu pilgrimage site featuring 52 bathing ghats and the rare Brahma Temple.

  • Highlights: Brahma Temple & Fair
  • Best Time: Nov (Fair)
  • Entry: Free
Heritage

Junagarh Fort

Bikaner

Junagarh Fort

An unconquered fort originally called Chintamani, featuring red sandstone walls and ornate palaces.

  • Highlights: Prachina Museum
  • Best Time: Oct - Mar
  • Entry: ₹50 (Indian)
Romantic

Lake Pichola

Udaipur

Lake Pichola

An artificial fresh water lake created in 1362 AD, famous for the floating Lake Palace.

  • Highlights: Sunset Boat Ride
  • Best Time: Sep - Mar
  • Entry: Free (Boat extra)
Legendary

Chittorgarh Fort

Chittorgarh

Chittor Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site and India's largest fort, symbolizing the Rajput spirit of valor and sacrifice.

  • Highlights: Vijay Stambh & Padmini Palace
  • Best Time: Oct - Mar
  • Entry: ₹40 (Indian)
