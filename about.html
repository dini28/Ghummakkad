Who We Are

Driven by Passion Built on Integrity

Ghummakkad Yaatri was founded in 2018 by two native Rajasthani travel enthusiasts with a simple, yet ambitious, vision: to redefine authentic travel in the desert state.

Starting as a small local tour guide service in Udaipur, our early focus was on heritage haveli stays and cultural immersion programs. Our commitment to showcasing the real Rajasthan quickly set us apart.

Today, we serve thousands of happy travelers globally. Our founding principle that every visitor deserves to feel like royalty in our home remains the driving force behind everything we do.
