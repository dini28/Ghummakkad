Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a tour package?

You can book directly through our website by selecting your desired package, or contact our support team via phone or email for a coherent custom itinerary.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your trip. For last-minute cancellations, a nominal fee may apply depending on hotel policies.

Do you provide custom travel itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in tailor-made experiences. Reach out to us with your preferences, and we will craft a Rajasthan journey just for you.

Yes, Rajasthan is very hospitable. We also provide trusted guides and verified accommodations to ensure maximum safety for all our guests.
