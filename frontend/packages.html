The Royal Rajasthan Tour - 7 Days

Day 1

Arrival in Jaipur

Pickup from airport. Check-in to heritage hotel. Evening visit to Chokhi Dhani for traditional dinner.

Day 2

Jaipur Sightseeing

Visit Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Jantar Mantar. Shopping in Johari Bazaar.

Day 3

Jaipur to Jodhpur

Drive to Jodhpur (5 hrs). Visit Pushkar Brahma Temple on the way. Check-in at Jodhpur.

Day 4

Blue City Exploration

Morning tour of Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Evening walk near Clock Tower market.

Day 5

Jodhpur to Udaipur

Drive to Udaipur via Ranakpur Jain Temples using the scenic route. Evening boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Day 6

City of Lakes Tour

City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon ki Bari, and Vintage Car Museum.

Day 7

Departure

Breakfast and transfer to Udaipur Airport for your onward journey.

Desert Mystique Safari - 4 Days

Day 1

Arrival in Jaisalmer

Transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Gadisar Lake and local markets.

Day 2

The Golden Fort

Explore Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila), Patwon Ki Haveli, and Jain Temples.

Day 3

Sam Sand Dunes

Drive to Sam Dunes. Camel safari at sunset. Cultural folk dance and dinner at desert camp.

Day 4

Departure

Check-out from camp and transfer to Jaisalmer Railway Station/Airport.

Lake City Romance - 3 Days

Day 1

Royal Heritage

Departure
